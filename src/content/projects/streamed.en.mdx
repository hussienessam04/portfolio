The first cut of Streamed was a Notion template. It worked for one director and
one director's mom. By the third filmmaker I had a real product on my hands.

The hard part wasn't the page builder — every page builder is fine. The hard
part was making "ship a page" take fewer steps than "open Premiere." You click,
type a slug, paste a Vimeo link. The page exists under your domain. You shared
the URL on Letterboxd, you went back to bed.

Everything else is implementation: Stripe for tickets (so festival directors
can monetize a single screening), a Postgres store so the team can audit who
has what page, and a small bento of preset layouts so the second page doesn't
look like the first.
